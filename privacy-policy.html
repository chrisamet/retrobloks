Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Summary: retrobloks does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is fully protected.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how retrobloks ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

retrobloks is designed to be a completely offline, privacy-respecting game. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect device identifiers
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not display advertisements
  • We do not require an internet connection
  • We do not require account creation or login

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • High Score: Your best score is saved so you can track your progress
  • Settings Preferences: Your chosen game settings (controls, sound, haptics, starting level)

This data is stored using Apple's standard UserDefaults system and never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to us or any third party. You can reset this data by deleting and reinstalling the app.

Third-Party Services

retrobloks does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. We do not use:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Crash reporting services
  • Cloud storage services

Children's Privacy

retrobloks is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy. The app is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through our app. All game data remains on your device under the protection of iOS security features.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this policy and the "Last Updated" date above. Any significant changes would be reflected in an app update, and we would never begin collecting personal data without clear notice and consent.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. If you wish to remove the locally stored high score and settings, simply delete the app from your device.
